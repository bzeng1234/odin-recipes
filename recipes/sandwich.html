Sandwich

Recipe on how to make a generic ham sandwich.

Ingredients

  • bread
  • ham
  • lettuce
  • mayo

Steps

  1. Add a bread
  2. Place ham on top of bread
  3. Place lettuce on top of ham
  4. Squeeze mayo on lettuce
  5. Finish by topping it off with bread
  6. Enjoy!
